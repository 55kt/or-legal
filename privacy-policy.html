Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OrApp ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

OpenAI API: Your questions are sent to OpenAI's servers to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy at openai.com/privacy. We send only the text of your question.

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.
