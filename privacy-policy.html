Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OrApp ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

OrApp is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here is what we process:

Questions you ask: When you submit a question, it is sent to a third-party AI service (OpenAI) to generate a response. We do not store your questions on our servers. Questions are processed in real-time and are not linked to your identity.

Subscription information: If you subscribe to Or Premium, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Usage data: We store a daily request counter locally on your device (using UserDefaults) to manage the free tier limit of 5 questions per day. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, device identifiers, or any other personal information. We do not create user accounts. We do not track you across apps or websites. We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. Third-Party Services

OpenAI API: Your questions are sent to OpenAI's servers to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes this data according to their own privacy policy, available at openai.com/privacy. We send only the text of your question — no personal identifiers are included.

Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases are handled by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions: apple.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage

All app data (daily request counter, subscription status) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store user data. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.

5. Children's Privacy

OrApp is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App contains AI-generated content that may not be suitable for minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: volossoftware@icloud.com
